Founders

Irene Gabashvili

Dr. Irene Gabashvili has extensive experience in biomedical research, software development and patient care.  She is committed to the development of next-generation technologies (sensors and AI) for optimal living, efficient healthcare delivery and microbiome-based therapies. A Stanford Medicine and HP Labs alumna, Dr. Gabashvili  has initiated and completed numerous flagship projects and lectured to both graduate and undergraduate students at universities worldwide.

Chris Allsup

Chris Allsup has extensive experience in sales and marketing of electronic design automation and test products, cloud-based design solutions and VoIP systems. He has worked with cutting-edge technologies ranging from high-level digital synthesis to analog physical synthesis and automatic test pattern generation. Chris earned a BSEE degree from UC San Diego and an MBA degree from Santa Clara University, and has authored numerous articles and papers about design, test and economics.
