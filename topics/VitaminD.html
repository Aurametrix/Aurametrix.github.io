Vitamin D

Group of fat soluble organic compounds known as the sunshine vitamin. It is produced by the body when skin is exposed to sunlight.

The most important types of vitamin D are vitamin D3 (also known as cholecalciferol) and vitamin D2 (ergocalciferol).

Vitamin D is available under the following different brand names: Drisdol, Calciferol, cholecalciferol, 1,25-Dihydroxycholecalciferol, and ergocalciferol.

Vitamin D is involved in calcium absorption, immune function, and protecting bone, muscle, and heart health. It's deficiency is very common. It is noticed in people with IBS, can include muscle weakness, pain, fatigue and depression.
