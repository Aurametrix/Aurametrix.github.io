Irene Gabashvili

Irene Gabashvili
Founder, Aurametrix

Dr. Irene Gabashvili has extensive experience in biomedical research, software development and patient care. She is committed to the development of noninvasive technologies for medical diagnostics,  next-generation sensors and Artificial Intelligence for Health IoT insights, data mining for efficient healthcare delivery, sustainable energy  and microbiome-based therapies. A Stanford Medicine and HP Labs alumna, Dr. Gabashvili has authored numerous publications, has led diverse projects and lectured to both graduate and undergraduate students at universities worldwide. She also serves as a director of MeBO Research and on the Parks & Recreation Commission of Sunnyvale, California.
