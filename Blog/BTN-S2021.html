September 9 update for Tellico Village

subscribe/unsubscribe   
Data is everywhere. But how reliable is it? This community-specific statistics is based on feedback from hundreds of Tellico Village residents.  

According to the latest results, we can estimate that a little over 15% of TV residents have survived COVID-19. At least 20% of these population are experiencing Long COVID symptoms. About 3% of residents overall got vaccinated after recovering from COVID-19. 

This means that about 75% of Tellico Village has some degree of protection against COVID-19  - from vaccination or past infection. 

The now-dominant Delta variant makes more people susceptible to COVID. Vaccine breakthrough cases are happening in the village right now (about 1 case per every 150 vaccinated). Based on our statistics, the chances of fully vaccinated people getting infected by the novel coronavirus are at least 1 in 100.

Only one of these breakthrough cases happened 8 months after full immunization, and all other infections occurred 1-5 months after the final dose of the vaccine. There were also cases in between vaccinations. 

About 15% of Tellico Village residents reported avoiding COVID-19 despite being exposed to confirmed cases. These individuals experienced minimal reactions to the vaccine.  Residents that took booster shots did not experience notable side effects from either of their shots. 

Stay Healthy. Stay Safe.
